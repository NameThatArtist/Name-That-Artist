# Name That Artist 🎵

A music artist guessing game. See blanked-out song titles, guess the artist in 5 tries.
**No server, no login, no data collection — just open and play.**

🎮 **Play online: [link will be added after deploy]**

---

## How to Play

1. Pick a category (decade or genre) — or choose **Random** for a mixed pack
2. You'll see blanked-out song titles from a mystery artist
3. Type the artist's name and hit **Guess Artist**
4. Wrong guesses unlock hints: letters revealed, debut year, genre, country
5. Guess the artist before you run out of tries!

---

## Pack Mode

Each category plays as a **pack** — every artist in that category, shuffled, back-to-back.

- Progress bar shows "Artist 3 / 15" as you go
- Results auto-advance to the next artist after 3 seconds (or click "Next Artist →")
- Final summary shows your grade (A+ → F), score, avg guesses, and best streak
- **Quit Pack** button shows partial results at any time
- **Random** mode picks 20 artists from the full pool
